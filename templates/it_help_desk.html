Frequently Asked Questions

How do I set up my Carleton email?

Your Carleton email (cmail.carleton.ca) is automatically created when you activate your MyCarletonOne account. Log in at mail.carleton.ca using your full Carleton email address and MyCarletonOne password.

I forgot my MyCarletonOne password. What do I do?

Visit Change Password or go to mycarletonone.carleton.ca and click "Forgot Password". You will need access to your recovery email or phone number to reset it.

How do I connect to eduroam Wi-Fi on campus?

Connect to the "eduroam" network. Enter your full Carleton email address as your username (e.g. janedoe@carleton.ca) and your MyCarletonOne password. Use WPA2 Enterprise with PEAP/MSCHAPv2.

How do I access licensed software (e.g. Microsoft 365)?

Carleton students get free Microsoft 365. Sign in at office.com using your Carleton email address. Other licensed software (SPSS, Adobe, etc.) is available through ITS Software Downloads in the portal.

My Carleton Central account is locked. How do I unlock it?

Contact the IT Help Desk by phone or walk-in with your student ID. For security reasons, account unlocks cannot be processed by email.
