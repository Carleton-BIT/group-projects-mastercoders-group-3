🎓 Program Requirements

Program: Bachelor of Information Technology – Information Resource Management (IRM)

Total Credits Required: 20.0

Offered jointly by: Carleton University & Algonquin College

Note: All courses in this program are required — there are no free electives. A minor in another academic discipline is mandatory.

The requirements below reflect the current calendar year. Contact your academic advisor if you were admitted under an earlier calendar.

First Year 4.0 credits

All of the following courses must be completed:

  • IRM 1008 – Introduction to Information Resource Management (0.5)
  • IRM 1002 – Reference and Information Services (0.5)
  • IRM 1003 – Collections Management (0.5)
  • IRM 1004 – Reader's Advisory Services (0.5)
  • IRM 1005 – Web Interface Development (0.5)
  • IRM 1006 – Subject Analysis and Indexing (0.5)
  • IRM 1007 – Cataloguing (0.5)
  • BIT 1400 – Introduction to Programming and Problem Solving (0.5)

Second Year 3.0 credits

All of the following courses must be completed:

  • IRM 2002 – Legal and Business Information (0.5)
  • IRM 2003 – Classification (0.5)
  • IRM 2004 – Information Management and Digital Preservation (0.5)
  • IRM 2005 – Advanced Cataloguing (0.5)
  • IRM 2006 – Data Visualization (0.5)
  • BIT 2009 – Statistics for Technology (0.5)

Third Year 3.0 credits

All of the following courses must be completed:

  • IRM 3001 – Scientific and Medical Information (0.5)
  • IRM 3003 – Legal Issues in Information Resource Management (0.5)
  • IRM 3004 – Project Management (0.5)
  • IRM 3006 – Data Analysis and Research Methodology (0.5)
  • IRM 3007 – Practicum for IRM (0.5)
  • IRM 3008 – Metadata for IRM (0.5)

Fourth Year 3.5 credits

All of the following courses must be completed:

  • IRM 4000 – Library Software (0.5)
  • IRM 4001 – Archives and Special Collections (0.5)
  • IRM 4002 – Network Technology (0.5)
  • IRM 4004 – Applied Machine Learning and Big Data Analytics (0.5)
  • IRM 4005 – Introduction to Deep Learning (0.5)
  • IRM 4900 – IRM Capstone Project (1.0)

Minor Requirement

All BIT IRM students must complete a minor in another academic discipline. The minor must be declared through the Registrar's Office and completed alongside program requirements.

Credits used to satisfy the minor may also count toward the 20.0 credit total where applicable. See your academic advisor for details.

GPA Requirement

A minimum cumulative GPA of 4.0 (equivalent to a C–) is required to graduate.

10.0 credits are included in the major CGPA; the remaining 10.0 credits are not included in the major CGPA calculation.
