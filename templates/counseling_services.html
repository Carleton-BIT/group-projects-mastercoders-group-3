About Counselling Services

Carleton's Counselling Services offers free, confidential mental health support to all registered students. Services are located in the Health and Counselling Centre (Room 2600, Herzberg Laboratories).

Hours: Monday–Friday, 8:30 AM – 4:30 PM

Phone: 613-520-6674

Individual Counselling

One-on-one sessions with a registered counsellor to address personal or academic concerns.

Same-Day Appointments

Same-day brief consultations are available for students in immediate need. Call or drop in at 8:30 AM.

Group Workshops

Workshops on stress management, mindfulness, exam anxiety, and more. No appointment needed.

Peer Support

Trained student volunteers offer peer-to-peer support through the Active Listening program.
